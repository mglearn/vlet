SOLO Taxonomy

Structure of Observed Learning Outcome

A research-based alternative to Bloom's that assesses the complexity of student understanding.

Prestructural

"I don't know what I don't know." Inaccurate or irrelevant responses.

Unistructural

Understanding one relevant aspect. Surface level.

Multistructural

Several relevant aspects known, but as separate pieces.

Relational

Integrating parts into a coherent whole. Deep learning.

Extended Abstract

Generalizing to new domains. Transfer learning.

PRISM Framework

Structured thinking for complex problem-solving and transfer learning.

P
Patterns

What recurring trends do you notice?

R
Reasoning

How do the pieces connect logically?

I
Ideas

Generating multiple approaches or perspectives.

S
Situation

Considering the broader context and implications.

M
Methods

Evaluating and verifying solutions.
